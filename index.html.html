I'm Aseer Khan, an automotive engineering student at HAN University of Applied Sciences. Mechanics, thermodynamics, propulsion — I'm drawn to all of it.

Jet engines, race cars, heavy machinery, military vehicles — anything built for raw performance or extreme conditions captivates me. Whether it's a fighter jet or a tank, I'm drawn to the engineering behind machines that operate at the absolute edge.

My studies have taken me from hands-on workshop experience and MATLAB modelling, to PLC programming and powertrain analysis. I thrive at the intersection of theory and the physical world.
